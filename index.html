Heavy Lead Graphite Wars

Once upon a time, there was an artist who was tired of their mechanical pencil breaking every time they needed to take notes. As they were cursing their graphite for breaking, a little wizard man appeared -poof- in front of them. I say! roared the wizard-man. If you really want a pencil whose graphite never tires, see if you're powerful enough to wield one first!

Defeat the magic pencil by reaching the finish line first. Click as quickly as you can, but beware! Click too fast and your graphite might break!
